First post

Posted on June 2nd 2021

Something excited happened when we arrived to London. We just got off the plane when a weird lady started to talk to us. We were afraid, because we did not know her initials intentions. She began to talk about how her daugther was supposed to attend a Harry Styles' concert but she got COVID-19 and was ill. So she was looking for a group of teens who would like to take their place. WE WERE SHOCKED!!!!!!! We accepted the tickets and give the woman a hug. We were extremely grateful for her kidness. It was the best concert ever. :)
